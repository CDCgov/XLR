Welcome

Welcome to the Accelerator Team (XLR) website and blog. Here we will post DevOps Collab presentations, events of interest and topics discussing open source technology and ideas. Please come here frequently as we will be posting DevOps collab presentations shortly after the meetings. Also, look for events and topics of interest which we will post periodically.

XLR Team

The Accelerator Team (XLR) is led by Jim Nasr mfi0@cdc.gov in OPHSS/CSELS/DHIS. The team was formed with the purpose of creating foundational microservices, libaries and tools that support programs throughout the agency. We espouse open source technology, distributed architecture, modern design principles and tools that allow us to move at the speed of thought.
